Attarintiki Daredi (2013) — Movie Review

(Genre: Action / Family Drama | Runtime: 168–170 min | Language: Telugu)

Rating

★★★★☆ 4.0 / 5

Synopsis: A fun family drama where Gautham (Pawan Kalyan) tries to reunite his estranged family. Filled with humor, emotion, and catchy songs.

Review: Attarintiki Daredi is an entertaining mix of comedy and sentiment, powered by Pawan Kalyan’s charm and strong supporting cast. The direction keeps the story engaging, though some moments feel predictable. A must-watch for fans of Telugu family entertainers.

Quick Info

Starcast: Pawan Kalyan, Samantha Ruth Prabhu, Pranitha Subhash;
Director: Trivikram Srinivas
Music: Devi Sri Prasad
